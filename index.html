Hiya, I'm Katy! I started Mourne Archery Centre in 2009 because I wanted to provide a service to my local community and I knew archery would be a great way to do that. I am passionate about keeping our sport accessible and affordable, about providing a space for kids and adults to learn new skills and meet new people. I'd love to have you down to the barn so I could show how we build community and have a lot of fun in the process.

The centre is based in a barn in the middle of Maghera, Co. Down - really close to Newcastle. Our normal sessions run for 3 hours with a variety of fun games ranging from traditional target shooting to popping balloons. We can have 15 shooters on the line at any one point and for larger groups we can take turns.

As well as providing a venue for one-off events, we also have an active and committed membership. We have dedicated nights for our members for competition, socialising and skill building. Members also get better rates when they are booking for their families and friends.

What do you need to do?

  1. Pick from above the session that suits you best.
  2. Make a booking request with the form below or call/text/Whatsapp 07519882372
  3. Pay via Paypal, bank transfer or bring exact cash.
  4. Arrive 25 minutes prior to your shooting time for safety brief and equipment allocation. Arrive 15 minutes beforey our shooting time if you have been with us before and remember your safety, for equipment allocation.

NB: Booking is essential. Max 5 people per booking for the 1.5hr session. Please being a glove to protect your fingers. We cannot offer refreshments due to Covid. Please feel free to bring your own.

Covid-19 Update

We are really excited to be able to start offering sessions again from the start of August - we don't have a firm date but we are working on it. We've missed you!

Obviously, we are really concerned to keep everyone as safe as possible. We will provide hand-sanitiser, clean our bows and surfaces between sessions and ask that you respect social distancing rules. Don't worry there is plenty of room in the barn!

We are going to have to limit groups to 6 and offer a shorter time shooting to leave time for cleaning and preparation. Sadly we won't be able to offer tea and coffee at the moment, so please bring your own drinks.

Initially, we can only offer sessions to over 12s but are working to change this as soon as possible.

Stay safe and see you soon!
